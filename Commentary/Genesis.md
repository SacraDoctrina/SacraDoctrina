## THE BOOK OF GENESIS

## STRUCTURE

these is the descendants(or generations) of X:
1. 2:4a:"these are the generations of the havens and the earth" (creation)
2. 5:1a:"these are the generations of Adam"
3. 6:9a:"these are the generations of Noah" (the flood)
4. 10:1a:"these are the generations of the sons of Noah"
5. 11:10a:"these are the generations of Shem"
6. 11:27a:"these are the generations of Terah" (Abraham story)
7. 25:12a:"these are the generations of Ishmael"
8. 25:19a:"these are the generations of Isaac" (Jacob story)
9. 36:1a:"these are the generations of Esau"
10. 37:2a:"these are the generations of Jacob" (Joseph story)

One type is the vertical genealogy, which traces one line of descrnt.
亚当 - 诺亚(Gen 5:1ff); 闪 - 亚伯拉罕 (Gen 11:10ff).
The second type of genealogy in Genesis is a horizontal or segmented type in which the genealogy is not traced through on son(the oldest), but throught various children.
闪/含/亚弗 (10:1ff); 以实玛利 (25:12); 以扫 (36:1,9)
