Hence we should say that divine wisdom holds the originals of all things, and these we have previously called the Ideas [ideas ], that is the exemplar forms of existing in the divine mind [formas exemplaries in mente divina existentes ]. … In this sense, therefore, God is the original exemplar of them all [Deus est primum exemplar omnium ].”(Summa Theologiae , 1a. 44, 3)
