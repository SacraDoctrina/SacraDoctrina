# Cultura Animi

cultura animi philosophia est.[^2]

It is by living – no, not living, but by dying and giving ourselves up to hell that we becomes theologians, not by understanding, reading, and speculating.[^8]

[^2]: Cicero, De Tusculanibus Quaestionibus, 8a, 11, 5, 18.
[^8]: Operationes in Psalmos (1519-21), in WA 5:163.28-29. Mattes, Luther’s Theology of Beauty, 34.
